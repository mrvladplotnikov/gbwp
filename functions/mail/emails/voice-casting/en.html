In this letter, we will tell you how much the voice casting costs in our agency and how the project budget is calculated.

The total cost depends on the choice of actor, the final sound requirements, and the number of words in the scenario.

First of all, you need to decide on the performer. You can listen to the available voices here. The cost of an hour of the voice talent’s work depends on the language:

Local voice actors: Ukrainian, Russian, English languages.
Price €225/hour.
Foreign voice actors: English, German, French, Spanish, Chinese.
Price: €360/hour.

If you have not found the desired language or timbre on the list of available voices, do not hesitate to email us about this, and we will provide you the right talent.

The hour of work includes recording an actor and the necessary sound processing. On average, it is possible to voice 800-1000 words of the scenario in one hour, but it all depends on the project’s specifics.

The total project budget is significantly influenced by the scenario and the number of words in it. To help you determine the right budget, we need to read the script.

The cost is also affected by additional sound processing and different sound effects. Here everything depends on your requirements, and it should be discussed separately.

The minimum project cost is the price of one hour of work.

We hope that now it has become more apparent to you how the project budget for voice-casting is calculated. If you have other questions, be sure to email us and we will be happy to help you!
